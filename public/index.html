Elevate Your CORE Habits

The CORE program is our "red carpet fitness experience". We take a foundational approach to fitness by establishing healthy habits based on the principles of nutrition. Everything we do is individualized and tailored to you and your lifestyle because there's no "one size fits all" approach to reaching your health goals. CORE stands for the four phases of our program - Cue, Observe, Repeat, Evaluate.

In the Cue phase, we'll help you identify the unhealthy behavior cues that have been holding you back and replace them with effective ones, setting the foundation for sustainable healthy habits that will last a lifetime.

During the Observe phase, we closely monitor how these changes are affecting your mood, body, and overall health. We use this information to fine-tune your approach, ensuring you get the most out of your efforts and achieve your goals.

The Repeat phase is where the real magic happens. We'll repeat what's working and replace what isn't, ensuring you continue to make progress towards your health goals.

Finally, in the Evaluate phase, we take stock of the cumulative benefits and successes you've experienced and set new goals to propel your progress even further. By following this comprehensive framework, you'll not only achieve short-term success but set yourself up for long-term health and wellness.
